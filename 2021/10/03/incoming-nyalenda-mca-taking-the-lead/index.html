Incoming Nyalenda MCA Taking The Lead

by admin · October 3, 2021

Earlier today incoming Nyalenda B MCA, HON SAMMY BOY joined faithful on Youth Day at KAG church where he encouraged the youths and shared with them the word of the lord from the book of Proverbs 24:10, which says, “If you faint in the days of adversity, your strength is small”. He shared his life story, growing up in the slums of Nyalenda, which the Honorable Leader describes as something that is tough upbringing, and despite all his adversities he managed to develop unique strength and abilities in order to cope with living in a high-stress environment.

HON SAMMY BOY, who is an entrepreneur running several business ventures in the slum offering employment to some of the jobless youths in the slum also encouraged youths not just to focus their mind so much on employment, but rather focus their mind on how they invest in opportunities and run there own business. He echoed this at a time that several graduates have put their profession aside and now doing odd jobs i.e riding bodaboda, working in mjengo sites and some also working in a carwash. He later wounded up by donating an undisclosed amount of money to the fundraising event that the church held. He also promised vibrant changes in the slum that he feels is his responsibility to change.
