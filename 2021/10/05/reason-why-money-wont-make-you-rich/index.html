Reason Why Money Won’t Make You Rich.

by admin · October 5, 2021

When you fall in love with money it posses you, that’s why money is not something to fall in love with instead one can choose to fall in love with God, family, friends, and neighbors. By itself, money does not truly make you rich. It is neutral. That is why we need to acquire moral wealth before acquiring a large amount of money. Before one can put all his energy into amassing monetary wealth, never ignore the development of your character.

I know many of you are now wondering, “what is this moral wealth?” To understand this, you must first know that wealth doesn’t affect you morally it magnifies what is already there. If you are an immoral poor person, it is most unlikely that you will gain morals upon gaining wealth. Morals are the social rules concerned with “Good” or “Bad”. Whereby good morals are those that people desire and approve in the society we live in, while bad morals are those that receive rejection in the society, it is, therefore, a clear indication that wealth only magnifies the real person you are. Therefore before putting all energy in amassing monetary wealth, one should first acquire moral wealth.

Acquiring monetary wealth before acquiring moral wealth leads to money slavery. When you become a slave to money, it starts to control you instead you controlling it. It happens if one doesn’t have any idea on how he can manage his wealth and riches, lacking this preparedness of how to manage your wealth, it may lead to that wealth “eating you alive” at no any given point in life should you let wealth rule over you, make money but never let money make you

So what is money without happiness? One crucial fact about money is that money will not make you happy. We use the money to solve certain problems, which may produce a temporary form of fulfillment or happiness but it’s never the source of joyful life-christ is. Did you know that the reason why many are not happy is that they’ve not understood the basic truths of life or they simply refuse to live by them? I thought you should know. The logic behind money that disqualifies it as a source of happiness is that the more you have the more you want, no amount will make you feel like you’re contented.

And lastly, money should bless its possessor and not kill him. Before you get in pursuit of financial prosperity, you first need to train yourself to be righteous in your heart and become prosperous in your soul. Whoever has righteousness is content, no matter how much money they have or not have, if you’re not contented with the little you have, there is no way you’re gonna attract abundance. The entire world’s wealth could never corrupt a righteous person because of the righteous rule over wealth and not the other way around. Money neither controls nor dominates them. They are free to use and channel it as God’s Spirit directs them without the fear of loss.
